# Palette's Journal - Critical UX & Accessibility Learnings

This journal records critical UX/a11y insights and patterns discovered during development.

## 2025-05-14 - [Initial Setup]
**Learning:** UX and accessibility are core to the DinoDiv landing page, requiring consistent use of Shadcn components and ARIA attributes even when placeholders are used as the primary visual label.
**Action:** Always provide screen-reader-only labels and proper ARIA relationships (invalid, describedby) for all form inputs.
